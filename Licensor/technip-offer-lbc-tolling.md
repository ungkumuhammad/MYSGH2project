## Comparison: Terminalling vs. Cracking key principles

| Section | Terminalling — Key principles | Cracking — Key principles |
|---------|-------------------------------|---------------------------|
| **Scope of the indicative offer** | Indicative fees and terms will be further developed in binding agreement. Only applies to the terminal services described below. | • NH₃ Cracking as a service under a **Tolling Agreement** with a Monthly Service Fee (incl. the initial investment, construction, operating and maintaining the ACU according to its capacity).<br>• Indicative fees and terms will be further developed into a first binding **Heads of Agreement**. Only applies to the Cracking Services described. |
| **Services covered** | • Unload refrigerated ammonia from vessels and throughput to re-export exit points or cracker.<br>• Load pressurized and (semi-)refrigerated NH₃ onto barges, coasters or midsize vessels, and load pressurized NH₃ onto trains. | The **ACU** will receive NH₃ from the connected LBC Ammonia Terminal and will deliver H₂ into the **Hynetwork H₂-Backbone**. |
| **Fee & capacity commitment \*** | • **40 (+/- 40%) EUR/t NH₃** throughput fee; **100% ToP** (take-or-pay).<br>• Yr 1 — not less than **300 ktpa**; by 2035, not less than **700 ktpa**.<br>• Additional capacity at discounted price. | **Monthly Cracking Service Fee (FMSV)** of 1/12 of **€50,000,000** — i.e. **€4,167,000 per month** — for a **Full Capacity Reservation of 100 ktpa**. |
| **Key conditions** | • **15 years** commitment with extension option (T&Cs to be negotiated).<br>• Title remains with customer. Operator has custody of the product.<br>• Break fee to apply for "binding capacity reservation" until firm contract executed.<br>• Early termination, incl. "no fault" termination.<br>• NH₃ specifications.<br>• Allowance for maintenance period.<br>• Physically commingled product; administrative segregation available. | • Minimum **15 years** commitment with option to extend.<br>• Title & risk of the products (NH₃ and H₂) remain with customer. Operator has custody.<br>• Uninterrupted feedstock supply considered.<br>• Break fee to apply for "binding capacity reservation" until final **Ammonia Cracking Service Agreement (ACSA)** is signed, taking several scenarios into account (incl. FID not achieved, no proceedings, no fault termination, …).<br>• NH₃-feedstock and H₂-offtake specifications (purity, flows, pressure and temperature) to apply. |
| **Others — General legal provisions** | Applicable law = **NL**; customer credit support; public announcement. | Applicable law = **NL**; parent guarantee; scheduled and unscheduled maintenance period; delivery points and title and risk; availability and reliability; yield; liability clause. |

\* Fees and capacities are **indicative** and subject to further development in the binding agreement(s).

### Glossary (as used on the slide)

- **ACU** — Ammonia Cracking Unit.
- **ACSA** — Ammonia Cracking Service Agreement.
- **FMSV** — (Fixed) Monthly Service/Cracking Fee.
- **ToP** — Take-or-Pay.
- **FID** — Final Investment Decision.
- **Hynetwork H₂-Backbone** — Dutch national hydrogen transport network (NL).
