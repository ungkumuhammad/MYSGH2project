1 FOREWORD
This document represents the Design Basis for a New Ammonia Cracking Unit (ACU) to
crack the ammonia into hydrogento bedelivered to onshore consumers or injected into the
existing hydrogen pipeline backbones.
The proposed Ammonia cracking facility and all required infrastructure is foreseen to be
located in Malesia.
The study foresees different ammonia cracking trains designed for four main nominal
capacities referred toproduced hydrogen:
o CASE 1:12 ktpa
o CASE 2: 24 ktpa
o CASE 3: 68 ktpa
o CASE 4: 80 ktpa.
Foreach scenario, three main alternatives have been required to be studied:
o CASE A -100% Natural Gas Fuel
o CASE B -50% Natural Gas Fuel / 50% Ammonia Fuel (Cracked Gas)*
o CASE C- 100% Ammonia Fuel(Cracked Gas)*
Notes:
*Ammonia fuel refers to cracked gasfuelresulting from the ammonia decomposition reaction. Refer tofor
detailed description“Process Descriptionand principles ofProcess control & safeguarding philosophy” Doc.
N. A23070S-E-PRZ-0002.
These alternatives differ in the use of different fuel gas sources.
Scope of this document is to define of the job specifications of the project.
