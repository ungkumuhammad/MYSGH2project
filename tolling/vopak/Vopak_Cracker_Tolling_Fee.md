● Vopak is the world's leading independent tank storage company, providing storage for
new energies & feedstocks, gas, industrial, chemical and oil products. Vopak acquired
a former refinery in Antwerp in May 2023 and rebranded it as Vopak Energy Park
Antwerp (VEPA). VEPA aims to become a green energy hub, focusing on ammonia,
methanol, and chemical imports. It has excellent transport connections, including deep-
sea, river, road, rail, and pipelines for various products. VEPA is currently developing
an ammonia import terminal for distribution as feedstock and for conversion into
hydrogen.
● Linde is a member of the Linde plc, which is a global industrial gases and engineering
company providing solutions, technologies, and services for a variety of industries with
a focus on sustainability, productivity, and de-carbonization. Linde has developed
significant expertise to provide high-quality solutions, technologies and currently
produces industrial gases, such as hydrogen, based on various technologies and
2. Recitals feedstocks and is actively scaling-up facility for industrial scale conversion of

● Customer, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Gentari Sdn. Bhd., is incorporated to pursue

● Lead Parties intend for Vopak to develop an ammonia import terminal and auxiliary
services including but not limited to storage and reloading of pressurized barges,
pressurized pipeline export, refrigerated barges, and rail tank cars (the “Ammonia
Terminal”). It will be a phased development, potentially through the use of project
financing, of up to 4 ammonia tanks in total of 88.000cbm each. Tank configuration and
sizing to be defined in subsequent engineering phases.
● Lead Parties intend for Linde to develop an ammonia cracker connected to the
Ammonia Terminal at Vopak Energy Park Antwerp (the “Ammonia Cracker”; each of
